Doris Corbett

LPCMH, NCC, DCC, M.Ed

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor of Mental Health, Board Certified with the National Board of Certified Counselors (NBCC), who has been working with children and families in various roles for the past 13 years.

All of my experience has provided me with an understanding for some of the challenges people face with relationships, child-rearing and different life circumstances that cause an imbalance within their emotions that impact their mind, body and spirit.

My Qualifications
